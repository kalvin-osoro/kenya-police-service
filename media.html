Major Branches

Administrative Police
Uaminifu na Haki (Integrity and Justice)

Some functions of the Administrative Police Service are:

  • Provide assistance to the public when in need
  • Maintain law and order
  • Provide border control and security
  • Provide specialized stock theft prevention services
  • Apprehension of offenders
  • Performance of any other duties as may be assigned by the Inspector General

    • General Service Unit.
    Utumishi Kwa Wote (Service for All)

    The Unit is established to support the following functions:

  • Providing ecurity to his Excellency the President, state houses/ lodges.
  • Providing security for selected foreign Airlines.
  • Providing security to vital installations.
  • Controlling rioters’ mobs and civil disturbance.
  • Carrying out anti-poaching operations and escort duties.
  • Containing banditry and cattle rustling.
  • Countering terrorism activities and insurgencies.

    • Kenya Police Service
    Utumishi Kwa Wote (Service for All)

    Core Functions Of The Kenya Police Include:

  • Maintenance of law and order.
  • Preservation of peace.
  • Protection of life and property.
  • Prevention and detection of crime.
  • Apprehension of offenders.
  • Enforcement of all laws and regulations with which it has been charged.

    • Directorate of Crimanal Investigations. Justice, Integrity and Impartiality

    Current functions of the Directorate include:

  • Collect and provide criminal intelligence.
  • Investigating serious crimes like homicide.
  • Maintain law and order.
  • Detect and prevent crimes.
  • Apprehend offenders.
  • Maintain criminal records.
  • Conduct forensic analysis.
  • Execute the directions given to the Inspector General.
  • Coordinate Country Interpol Affairs.
  • Perform any other function conferred on it by other written Law.

    • Kenya Prisons
    Kurekebisha Na Haki (Rehabilitation and Justice)

    The core functions of the Kenya Prison Service include:

  • Keeping in safe custody all prisoners placed under its care.
  • Rehabilitation and reformation of prisoners.
  • Facilitation of Administration of Justice.
  • Containing and Rehabilitation of young offenders in Borstal Institutions and Youth Corrective Training Centre.
  • Provision of care for children aged 4 years and below accompanying their mothers to prison.
