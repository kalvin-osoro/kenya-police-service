Logistics

Functions

  1. Coordinating and preparation of policies. Plans and works for entire police service.
  2. Preparation and submission of annual procurement work plan
  3. Repairing and maintaining police infrastructure and support facilities
  4. Provision of general stores
  5. Liaison with police formations/provinces and sub-branches on procurement
  6. Replacement, modification of systems equipment and plants.
  7. Coordinating and publication of all police stationeries, manuals and pamphlets
  8. Provision and kitting all officers to laid down scales
  9. Collection and storage of data on all police logistic requirement
  10. Coordinating with the chief force armourer, chief transport officer, chief communication officer, procurement section and relevant government ministries to ensure that items needed in the field are readily available
  11. Management of the movement and storage of logistic materials
  12. Planning, implementing and controlling the efficient flow of goods and services
  13. Ensuring availability of goods/services requisitioned for and ensuring high level of customer service and satisfaction
