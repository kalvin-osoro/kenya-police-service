Diplomatic Police Unit

This unit was formed by the Kenya Police to support the diplomatic and UN community. They will usually respond to the scene of a crime or incident where members of Diplomatic community are involved. They provide Security advice on request

Their headquarters are currently located at Gigiri area off UN Avenue

Functions:

  1. Providing specialized security for Diplomats.
  2. Liaising with Diplomatic missions on security matters.
  3. Coordinating all rapid responses.
  4. Providing static and mobile security for Diplomatic missions.
  5. Foot and mobile security patrols.
  6. Traffic control and Accidents Investigation.
  7. Traffic control/Accidents Investigation.
