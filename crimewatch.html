CRIME WATCH

How the program works

Crime watch programs encourage neighbors to get to know one another, making it easier to identify a car or person that is out of place. Doing this can also help neighbors feel more comfortable reporting a problem or sharing concerns with one another. In addition to patrols, neighborhoods with watch programs are typically marked by signs that let people know the area is being monitored.

How to start a crime watch

  • Contact your neighbors
  • Identify concerns
  • Create a plan
  • Establish a schedule
